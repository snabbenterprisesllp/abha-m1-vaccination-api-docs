# About Us
## Snabb Enterprises LLP

---

## Company Overview

Snabb Enterprises LLP is an Indian technology company focused on developing healthcare solutions that improve access to and management of preventive healthcare services. Our primary focus is on digitizing childhood immunization records and supporting parents, hospitals, and clinics in maintaining accurate vaccination schedules.

We operate in compliance with Indian healthcare regulations and international health standards, with a commitment to data privacy, security, and user consent management.

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a secure, accessible, and compliant digital platform for managing childhood vaccination records from birth through 18 years of age. We aim to eliminate barriers to vaccination record management while ensuring alignment with national and international immunization guidelines.

We believe that accurate, accessible vaccination records are fundamental to public health and individual well-being. Our platform serves as a bridge between parents, healthcare providers, and national health infrastructure.

---

## The Platform

### Digital Vaccination Record and Reminder Platform

The Digital Vaccination Record and Reminder Platform is a comprehensive healthcare technology solution designed to address critical challenges in childhood immunization management in India.

The platform enables:

- Digital storage and management of vaccination records for children aged 0 to 18 years
- Age-based vaccination schedule generation aligned with Indian Universal Immunization Program (UIP) and World Health Organization (WHO) guidelines
- Automated reminders for upcoming vaccinations through multiple notification channels
- Secure document storage for vaccination certificates and related medical documents
- Hospital and clinic verification of vaccination records
- Integration readiness with national health infrastructure through ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account)

## Compliance and Standards

### Regulatory Alignment

Our platform is designed to comply with:

- Indian Universal Immunization Program (UIP) vaccination schedules
- World Health Organization (WHO) immunization recommendations
- Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) technical specifications
- Data privacy and security requirements for healthcare applications
- Consent management standards for health data sharing

### ABDM M1 Sandbox Participation

Snabb Enterprises LLP is currently participating in the ABDM M1 Sandbox program. This participation allows us to:

- Test integration capabilities with ABDM infrastructure
- Validate technical implementation of ABHA linking functionality
- Ensure compliance with ABDM consent and data sharing protocols
- Prepare for future production deployment after sandbox validation

**Important**: Our current platform operates exclusively in sandbox/testing mode. No real patient data is processed or stored in this phase. All ABHA integration testing is conducted using test data only.

## Data Privacy and Security

### Our Commitment

Data privacy and security are fundamental to our platform design and operations. We implement the following principles:

**Explicit Consent**
- All data sharing operations require explicit user consent
- Consent forms clearly explain purpose, scope, and duration
- Users retain the right to revoke consent at any time
- Consent decisions are logged for audit purposes

**Data Minimization**
- We collect only data necessary for vaccination record management
- Data is used solely for stated purposes
- No secondary use of data without additional consent

**Security Measures**
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit
- Industry-standard encryption for data at rest
- Role-based access control for all system operations
- Regular security audits and updates
- Secure authentication mechanisms

**Transparency**
- Clear privacy policies and terms of service
- Audit logging for all data access and modifications
- User access to their own data and consent records

## User Groups

### Parents and Guardians

Parents use our platform to create and manage digital profiles for their children, access complete vaccination histories, receive automated reminders, and maintain secure digital storage of vaccination certificates. The platform provides tools for tracking vaccination schedules and generating immunization reports for school admission, travel, and medical consultations.

### Hospitals and Clinics

Healthcare providers use our platform to record vaccinations with verification, access beneficiary vaccination histories with proper consent, and maintain accurate immunization records. The platform supports QR code-based patient identification and hospital-stamped vaccination records for accountability.

### System Administrators

Administrators manage hospital and clinic registrations, oversee user accounts and access controls, monitor system operations, and maintain compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Technical Foundation

### Architecture

Our platform is built using modern, secure technologies:

- Backend API: FastAPI (Python) with RESTful endpoints
- Database: PostgreSQL for structured data storage
- Cache: Redis for performance optimization
- Storage: Google Cloud Storage for document management
- Authentication: JWT-based authentication and authorization
- Frontend: React/Next.js for web applications, Flutter for mobile applications

### Security Infrastructure

- TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
- Secure token storage mechanisms
- API rate limiting and input validation
- Regular security assessments and updates
